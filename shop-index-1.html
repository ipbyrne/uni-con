Personal Shopper

Book a “Personal Shopper” appointment by calling us at the store on 408-564-0722 and join one of our Retail Assistants on Skype video to select from our large range of items – including the crystals.  You can book a 15 min, 30 min or 45 min Personal Shopper appointment during the hours of 1-4pm Wednesday thru Saturday or other times by special arrangement.

You will be able to see and select each of your items via Skype video, pay for them during your Personal Shopper call and we will promptly ship them to you via USPS via Flat Rate Parcel Service, to anywhere in the U.S.
