Welcome to The Universal Connection Store where our commitment is to bring balance, healing, love and light into homes all around the world by creating a space where support, education and tools are readily available for all, creating harmony, peace and joy.

Situated in the heart of beautiful Downtown Willow Glen, The Universal Connection sells a wide variety of new age, metaphysical goods. Our range includes Crystals, Handmade Crystal Jewelry, Books, CD's, Candles, Essential Oils, Incense and an array of decorative items.

With a range of practitioners available, we offer Healings, Reiki, Life Coaching, Card and Energy Readings, Angel work and Animal Communication just to name a few. As well as evening workshops and classes from Guided Meditations to Vision Board Creation to Full Moon Drumming Circles.

The Universal Connection Store is open 7 days a week. Whether you are calling forth your perfect crystal, looking for a beautiful gift or just deserving some peace and tranquility in your day, we are here to serve you.
